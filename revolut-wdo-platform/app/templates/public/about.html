About Revolut & WDO

Bridging the gap between citizens and policymakers through technology

Our Mission

To inspire participatory governance in African countries through leveraging data-driven analytics sourced from communities to inform policy transformation, enable enhanced public decision-making, and enhance government responsiveness.

Our Vision

A future where every citizen's voice contributes to shaping policies that affect their lives, creating more responsive and accountable governance across Africa.

How We Work

Multi-Channel Input

Collect feedback via USSD, SMS, mobile app and web platform to reach everyone.

Data Analytics

Advanced analytics identify trends and prioritize issues using sentiment analysis.

Policy Impact

Present insights to policymakers to drive data-informed decision making.

Our Team

We are a diverse team of technologists, policy experts, and civic engagement specialists committed to improving governance in Africa.
